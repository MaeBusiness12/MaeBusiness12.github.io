Overview

Find It is a game that allows the player to search for hidden items throughout five different pictures and areas.There are 5 different levels that become more difficult as the player passes them. The hidden items will become smaller and harder to find, often disapearing within the cluttered room. Find It is a perfect game to challenge your brain, while also providing a comforting, cozy, and relaxing game experience.

How to Play

When the player clicks start, a list of photos will appear under the larger picture. The player must:

  1. See what items need to be found.
  2. Find the items within the big picture.
  3. Finish the level in as little time as possible.
